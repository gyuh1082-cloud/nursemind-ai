How Nurses Think

Nurses do more than react to symptoms. They look for patterns, monitor subtle changes, identify safety risks early, and decide what needs attention first. This project was designed to reflect that kind of clinical reasoning.

About the Creator

I am a student interested in becoming a nurse practitioner. I built NurseMind AI to explore how technology can support patient safety, nursing judgment, and education.
