NurseMind AI 🩺

Clinical Risk & Nursing Priorities Simulator

What This Project Does

NurseMind AI simulates how nurses assess patient safety risks, prioritize care, and provide patient education.

Example Scenario

Patient: 78 years old, dizzy, low blood pressure

Risks:

  • Fall risk
  • Low blood pressure concern

Nursing Actions:

  • Assist patient when walking
  • Monitor blood pressure

Education:

  • Stand slowly
  • Ask for help when walking
