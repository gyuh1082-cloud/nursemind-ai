Clinical Risk & Nursing Priorities Simulator

💡 Project Purpose

I built this project to simulate how nurses assess patient safety, identify risks, and prioritize care.

🧪 Example Patient Case

Patient: 78 years old, dizziness, low blood pressure

⚠️ Risk Flags

  • Fall risk
  • Low blood pressure concern

👩‍⚕️ Nursing Actions

  • Assist patient when walking
  • Monitor blood pressure

📚 Patient Education

  • Stand slowly
  • Ask for help before walking

🧠 Why This Matters

Nursing is not just about treating patients, but identifying risks early and preventing harm. This project reflects my interest in becoming a nurse practitioner.
