NurseMind AI 🩺

Advanced Clinical Risk, Patient Safety, and Nursing Priorities Simulator

I built NurseMind AI to reflect how nurses think in real patient care settings: noticing early warning signs, protecting patient safety, prioritizing what matters most, and educating patients clearly. This project combines my interest in becoming a nurse practitioner with my interest in technology and human-centered healthcare.

Why This Project Matters

This is not just a basic health app. It is designed to simulate how nurses assess risk, monitor instability, respond to warning signs, and teach patients in ways that help prevent harm. I wanted this project to show that nursing is both compassionate and analytical.
